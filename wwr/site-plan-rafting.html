Overview

Purpose

Establishing an online presence for my rafting business is to attract more customers and increase bookings by providing information about our services and creating a platform for easy reservations.

Audience

Our primary target audience for the water rafting website consists of adventure enthusiasts and outdoor lovers who seek thrilling experiences in natural settings. Questions: Question: What is the age range of our target audience? Answer: Our Customers should range from 18 to 45 years old, encompassing individuals who are physically active and seeking exciting outdoor adventures. Question: Where do our target customers typically reside or come from? Answer: Our customers primarily come from urban and suburban areas, with a significant percentage traveling from nearby cities and tourist destinations. Question: What level of rafting experience are our target customers likely to have? Answer: Our customers may have varying levels of rafting experience, from beginners looking for introductory trips to experienced rafters seeking challenging whitewater expeditions.
